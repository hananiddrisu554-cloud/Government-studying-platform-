Meaning of Government

Government is the system or group of people that rule a country and make laws. In Ghana, the government operates under a democratic system.

The Three Arms of Government in Ghana

  • Executive: Headed by the President. Implements laws.
  • Legislature: Parliament. Makes laws.
  • Judiciary: Judges and courts. Interprets laws.

The 1992 Constitution of Ghana

The 1992 Constitution is the supreme law of Ghana. It establishes the structure of government and protects fundamental human rights.
